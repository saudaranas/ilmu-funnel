Brunson — Value Ladder Formula
1
Lead Magnet (RM0)
Ebook, checklist, mini-course percuma. Tujuan: dapat email.
2
Tripwire (RM19–RM49)
Produk entry-level. Tukar prospect jadi buyer pertama kali.
3
Core Offer (RM197–RM497)
Produk/kursus utama anda. Deliver transformasi penuh.
4
Profit Maximizer (RM997+)
Coaching, mastermind, done-for-you. Ini tempat profit sebenar.
